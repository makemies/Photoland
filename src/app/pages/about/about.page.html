About Us


Photoland is a picture application founded in 2022 as a school project. The application was developed by Henrik Säkkinen, Markus Pantti, Anton Mäyrä. The idea of ​​the application is to bring different pictures for users to view and brighten up their days. The pictures are divided into categories, everyone will surely find something they like to look at.
